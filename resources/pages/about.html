What IT's About?

This Server will act as a platform to facilitate communication and efficiency within the summer IT Crew. There are intended to be utilities to assist in tracking inventory and locating items within the server room, and to log questions and answers for easier troubleshooting.

How to use it...

The chat page can be used to keep in touch with everyone on the crew. Phones should not be used at any time. The help page will have documents and tutorials, along with frequent issues and their solutions. You can also submit an issue and a response will be supplied and a notification will appear on the screen.
